RULES AND GUIDE

 

In the FanZone we will host your own sprites, you can send them individually or you can ask for a custom gallery where you can have all of your sprites, just try to follow these rules

  • - For individual sprites, mail them attached or with a working URL to cardgallery@gmail.com specifying your nick and the name of the sprite, these sprites will be added to the Galería de Usuarios (Users Gallery)

  • - To ask for a custom gallery send a mail to the above address specifying;

    • Nick

    • Homepage (optional)

    • E-mail (optional)

    • 50x50 Avatar (optional)

    When you want to add a sprite to your custom gallery, send it to our mail specifying your nick (for possible e-mail changes)

  • - Offensive material or spam is not allowed

  • - You cand send any kind of sprite, but it's better if you follow the style of the site

  • - Plagiarism is easily detected, be just and don't overtake other people's work
